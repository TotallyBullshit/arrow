xAsComplex

Usage

xAsComplex(ims)

xAsComplex...(...)

Arguments

ims
a collection of complex values. A list, pairlist or vector of length-one complex vectors to convert to a complex vector.
...
see above.

Value

A complex vector.

Description

Convert a collection to a complex vector.

Details

xAsComplex converts a list, pairlist or vector of length-one complex numbers to a complex vector. It does not attempt to convert non-complex collections to complex vectors.
